COMPASS USA

Explore the possibilities

An interactive tool that helps associates
discover career opportunities

 
 

  • Overview

    The leader in Foodservice management and support services, Compass Group USA offers many different job roles across a variety of markets.

  • Challenge

    To help associates appreciate career growth opportunities and build their career paths at Compass group

  • Solution

    Collega creates an interactive tool that helps associates explore career options. Individuals at each level learn about advancement opportunities or discover roles in other sectors.

    Viewable on a PC or iPad, the tool is accessed on altogethergreat, the employee SharePoint site developed by Collega.
